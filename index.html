Your game title

""Amidais: Where did I begin""
  • High Concept: Escape the dog pit you were placed in by wall jumping
  • Player Experience Goals: I want to provide players with decent wall jumping mechanics.
  • Genre: Platformer
  • Game play: Player can wall jump by pressing up and towards the wall. Players must switch between walls to get to chain wall jumps. I plan to use these mechanics to have a larger level with obstacles.
  • Prototype goal: Wall jumping!
  • Features: You can jump off walls.
  • Setting/Premise: You are Amidais, a dog poorly designed by someone who thought non transparent sprite sheets were funny. You must escape and ask your creator... why did you make me so??
  • Story: Left right left right left ri- oh you fell out of the level.
  • Target Audience: Dogs.
  • Play time: 30 seconds
  • Strategies: Alternating walls between jumps.
  • Prototype Assets: I borrowed the terrible dog from http://www.dickbaldwin.com/XNA/XNA0122/XNA0122.htm whom has no idea who made the sprites. "I have a sneaking suspicion it is from harvest moon"
