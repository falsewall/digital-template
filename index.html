Phaser guy steroid simulator

  • High Concept: Two phaser men fight for the gold. Only one can leave alive.
  • Player Experience Goals: Enjoy your favorite phaser model on top of a beefed out sprite I made
  • Genre: fighter
  • Game play: Player 1 uses arrow keys to move and control to punch. Player 2 uses wasd to move and q to punch.
  • Prototype goal: Get ok animations down with the fighting.
  • Features: Sound signifies when you hit the opponent, keeping you from having to look at the gui.
  • Setting/Premise: The setting is a secret underground vault with gold and treasure inside. Both phaser guys want it, so they fight to the death.
  • Story: Indiana Phaser and Guy Jones stumble across a bunker full of treasure. Little do they know it is cursed with a curse of greed. Unable to share the loot, they battle to the death in honorable hand to hand combat.
  • Target Audience: People that use the phaser guy model in their games.
  • Play time: 2 minutes
  • Strategies: Punch your opponent at the right distance
  • Prototype Assets: I got my sound from http://www.acoustica.com/files/aclooplib/Sound%20Effects/ Everything else I made myself. Aside from phaser guys head.
