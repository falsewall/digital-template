Panda love making simulator

  • High Concept: Pandas need to reproduce. This is how it happens.
  • Player Experience Goals: Guide the player through panda love.
  • Genre: Platformer
  • Game play: Player can wall jump by pressing up and towards the wall. Collect all the hearts in the level to begin panda breeding.
  • Prototype goal: Make a level with randomly placed hearts and make a panda pick them up.
  • Features: You can jump off walls.
  • Setting/Premise: You are Amidais, a dog poorly designed by someone who thought non transparent sprite sheets were funny. You must escape and ask your creator... why did you make me so??
  • Story: You are Ming, the last panda of your kind in the area. You must reproduce or become the last of your kind. Collect all of the panda hearts to bring your panda family back from the brink of extinction.
  • Target Audience: Pandas
  • Play time: 2 minutes
  • Strategies: Alternating walls between jumps.
  • Prototype Assets: I got my panda sprite from http://opengameart.org/ and music from http://www.newgrounds.com/audio/listen/607189
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Mario
