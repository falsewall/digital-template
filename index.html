Phone finding simulator.

  • High Concept: Play breakout, click phones that ring
  • Player Experience Goals: Find your phone, don't lose breakout
  • Genre: arcade
  • Game play: Play breakout, click phones that ring.
  • Prototype goal: Have phones on a timer.
  • Features: Phones that are animated
  • Setting/Premise: West Philly, you are a Cat lady
  • Story: You are Eustice, the neighbourhood cat lady. Your cats have a habit of coming back home with stolen phones that look like yours. You have forgotten what your phone looks like and now due to infernal phone locks your only chance to find it is to answer incoming calls on the phones. Too bad your addicted to breakout.
  • Target Audience: Cats
  • Play time: 1 minutes
  • Strategies: Answer phones before 6 seconds of ringing passes
  • Prototype Assets: I got my phone sprite from www.xda-developers.com
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: breakout
