Canoeing Simulator

  • High Concept: Use your limited control over acceleration to make your way through a lighting bolt filled adventure to the pier.
  • Player Experience Goals: Enjoy the movement.
  • Genre: Side scroller.
  • Game play: Alternate the left and right keys to accelerate your boat. Don't hit lightning.
  • Prototype goal: Make the game side scroll.
  • Features: Lightning objects scattered throughout the backdrop on timers to strike.
  • Setting/Premise: Ocean setting with a canoeing main character.
  • Story: Your name is Noby Bick, an avid ocean canoe-er. You one day decide not the check the weather and find yourself stranded in the ocean during some stormy weather. Get back to the pier, but try not to get fried.
  • Target Audience: Avid canoers and meteorologists.
  • Play time: 3 minutes
  • Strategies: Dont move too fast.
