Finding your phone while playing breakout simulator

  • High Concept: Breakout gameplay paired with needing to move your cursor to visual cues.
  • Player Experience Goals: Lose extra due to added visual stimuli.
  • Genre: Hybrid
  • Game play: Play breakout, note only the phone answering is important for winning, but you will lose lives if you dont answer a phone in time or lose a ball.
  • Prototype goal: Make timers for phones and have them go off at random times.
  • Features: Phones are animated
  • Setting/Premise: West Philly, your a cat lady.
  • Story: You are an old cat lady whos cats tend to come back with stolen phones in mouth. In your senility you have forgotten which of the phones is yours and they are all locked. The only way to tell is to answer them when someone calls. Too bad you are addicted to playing breakout.
  • Target Audience: Cats
  • Play time: 2 minutes
  • Strategies: Don't wait more than 8 seconds to answer a phone or you will lose a life.
  • Prototype Assets: I got my phone sprite from www.xda-developers.com
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Breakout
